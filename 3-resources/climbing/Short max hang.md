# Short Max Hang
The goal is to find a weight that I can hold for 5 to 12 seconds. Cycle with long max hangs training if plateauing.
## Plan
- Up to 5 sets (each with different grip type)
- 3-5 reps per sets
- 2-3 minutes rest between reps
